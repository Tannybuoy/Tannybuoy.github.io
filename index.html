HealthTech Startup • 2023

Patient Engagement Platform Redesign The App Doctors Hate But Patients Actually Use

Orchestrated end-to-end redesign of patient-facing mobile application, driving a comprehensive UX overhaul informed by 200+ user interviews. Collaborated with clinical stakeholders to ensure HIPAA compliance while improving appointment scheduling, medication tracking, and telehealth integration.

Deutsche Bank • 2022

Client Analytics Dashboard & Risk Assessment Tool Excel Sheet That Became a "Platform"

Drove development of a real-time analytics dashboard for the Global Markets division, consolidating disparate data sources into unified risk visualization. Led requirements gathering across trading, compliance, and technology teams to deliver an integrated solution reducing decision latency.

Someone's 47-tab Excel workbook was "the system" for risk analysis. My job was to turn it into an actual dashboard without breaking the 15 macros that nobody understood but everyone relied on. Three months in, I discovered one macro was written by someone who left in 2019. We still don't know what it does, but removing it breaks everything. We kept it.

Manufacturing Co. • 2021

IoT-Enabled Predictive Maintenance System Teaching Machines to Complain Before They Break

Spearheaded IoT integration initiative connecting 500+ factory floor sensors to a centralized monitoring platform. Defined product requirements for predictive maintenance algorithms, enabling proactive equipment servicing and reducing unplanned downtime through data-driven decision making.

Put sensors on factory machines so they could tell us they were about to break instead of just... breaking. Sounds simple until you realize half the machines are from 1997 and communicate via protocols nobody documents anymore. Spent a month convincing floor managers that "the computer knows best" while secretly knowing the model had a 73% confidence interval. We called it "AI-powered" in the pitch deck.

40% Reduction in unplanned downtime Fewer "oh no" moments on the factory floor
